About JODOUR

Roots as a
Living
System.

JODOUR is a contemporary Moroccan design brand that blends ancestral craftsmanship with modern technology. Our mission is to transform traditional materials, techniques, and cultural symbols into functional, collectible, and timeless design objects.

Mission

What We Do

We create limited-edition design objects that merge Moroccan heritage with contemporary innovation. Our collection transforms traditional materials like clay, wood, copper, and brass into functional, artistic, and modern pieces.

Each product is a bridge between past and present, combining ancestral craftsmanship with modern technology. We build objects that are timeless, expressive, and deeply rooted in cultural identity.

Vision

Where We Go

To position Moroccan design at the forefront of global contemporary culture — not as nostalgia, but as a living, forward-looking design language that resonates with the world.

JODOUR aims to celebrate Moroccan identity while projecting it into the future, offering design objects that connect past knowledge with present needs and forward-looking vision.

Values

Our Principles
